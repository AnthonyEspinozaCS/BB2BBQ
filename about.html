About Us

A Household Name

Serving the Las Vegas community for over 10 years, we take pride in being someone you can trust. We're a comprehensive, mobile BBQ cleaning & repair service. Our experienced technicians ready to fix that broken ignition, and deep clean those grates.

Deep Clean

Your household's grill can harbor dirt, stuck-on food, and germs. All of these things can reduce the quality of your food, and even make your family sick. There's a common misconception that you can simply burn all of these things off. The reality is that grills that are not properly cleaned and maintained can accumulate bacteria, mold, rodent droppings, rust, and grease. Grease fires are a silent threat that can lead to a dangerous situation for your home and family. Our technicians make sure this situation is something you never have to think about.

Grill Repair

From the grates to the valves and everything in between, let our knowledgeable technicians handle the details. We'll start with an inspection, and thoroughly diagnose the necessary repairs. General maintenance is vital to a grill's safety and longevity, we'd love to the opportunity to show you we can bring your gril Back 2 New.
